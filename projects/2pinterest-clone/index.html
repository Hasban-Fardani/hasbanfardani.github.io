Overview

I have engaged in numerous competitive programming challenges across platforms such as

  • Codewars
  • HackerRank
  • LeetCode
  • TLX TOKI Indonesia

This experience has allowed me to tackle a variety of algorithmic problems, enhancing my coding proficiency and analytical thinking.

Problem

In software development and technical interviews, strong problem-solving skills are essential. Recognizing this, I began practicing competitive programming. It has helped sharpen my logic, speed, and confidence in coding.

Goal

  • Improve analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Deepen understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Write efficient, accurate code
  • Prepare for coding interviews and technical assessments

Solution

Improving Problem-Solving Skills

  • Practice Regularly: Build consistency and long-term understanding
  • Analyze Solutions: Compare with other approaches after solving
  • Study Concepts: Focus on theory and its practical application

Selecting Problems to Solve

  • By Difficulty: Start from easy, then increase complexity
  • By Topic: Focus on key areas like DP, graphs, recursion, etc.
  • By Relevance: Choose problems useful for interviews and real-world scenarios

Additional Strategies

  • Join Contests: Timed events improve focus and speed
  • Take Notes: Summarize tricks, patterns, and key takeaways
  • Community Engagement: Share solutions, discuss approaches, learn from others

Challenges and Learnings

  • Time Management: Balancing consistent practice with other responsibilities
  • Technical Difficulties: Breaking down and understanding hard problems
  • Motivation: Staying consistent over time

From these, I learned to plan better, study smarter, and persist even when progress is slow.

Final Thoughts

Competitive programming continues to help me grow as a developer. It strengthens core skills that are essential in both academic and professional environments. With consistent effort, this practice remains one of the most effective ways to build coding confidence.
